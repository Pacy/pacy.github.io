Studysmarter

Project at "Studysmarter" StartUp as part of the University program

No preview available

No picture of the original version saved/found

Responsible for creating and maintaining the front end part of the flashcard components prior to and after the launch of the web application.

The flashcard component was pulling the users selected flashcards from the server to test their knowledge and report their progress back.

The design of the component was according to ideas and feedback of the supervisor and own implementation. The flashcard was responsive based on the screen size and could include text as well as an image in the question and answer part of it. A transition animation, when a new card was shown, rounded up the user experience to simulate a new card being put on the stack of already answered ones.

Futhermore, buttons to reflect the user's knowledge of the flashcard and a general opinion of it could be sent to the server.

Music Me

Platform to connect teacher and students to learn music instrument (a four person university group project)

The project goal was to develop a business idea and implement the web application for it. (First contact with web application development for the whole team) We decided on a platform to connect users with potential teachers specifically in regard to music lessons.

There are no clear divisions for responsibilities & contributions in the development of the application, as everyone was involved in the whole application and its improvement. Therefore, it is hard to narrow down my own contribution to the whole project.
Contribution included:

  • Adjustments and fixes to the whole project
  • Creating and adding of a logo
  • Adding the message component
